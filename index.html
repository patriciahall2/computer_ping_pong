Let's play ping-pong!

How to play:

1.Type a number in the input field then press enter.
2. If the number is divisible by 3 then you get a "ping".
If the number is divisible by 5 you get a "pong". If the number is divisible by 15 then you get a "pingpong".
3.The number you enter will result in the display of each number counting up to the number you chose and show which numbers in the sequence are "ping", "pong", or "pingpong".
4. This game can help you refine and test your skills in division. Can you predict what the sequence will be?
